SAM College of Engineering and Technology (SAMCET)was established in the year 2007. The institute is approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE) and Government of India, New Delhi. SAMCET is affiliated to Barkatullah University, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education, Bhopal and Makhanlal Chaturvedi Rashtriya Patrakarita Avam Sanchar Vishwavidhyalaya, Bhopal. The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Management and Engineering.

SAM College of Engineering and Technology (SAMCET) Admission
Eligibility Criteria
Bachelor of Engineering (BE):
  • 10+2 (HSC) or its equivalent with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in PCM.
  • Obtained a valid score in the following exams:
    1. JEE Main (Joint Entrance Exam)
    2. PE and PT (Pre-engineering and Pharmacy Test)
Diploma:

10+2 (HSC) in science stream with Mathematics.

Master of Technology (M.Tech):
  • BE/B.Tech with a minimum of 50% marks in the relevant discipline.
  • Obtained a valid score in GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test of Engineering)
Master in Business Administration (MBA):
  • Graduate with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in any discipline.
  • Obtained a valid score in MET (Manipal Entrance Test).

Master in Computer Application (MCA):
  • Graduate with Mathematics as a relevant subject with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in any discipline.
  • Obtained a valid score in MP Pre MCA (Pre-Master of Computer Application).

Registration Process

Applicants are required to go through a state-level counseling process conducted by DTE, Madhya Pradesh. Steps for the same is mentioned below:

Step 1: Register for counseling through the website of DTE, MP and fill the online application form.

Step 2: Online verification of documents:
  • Applicants are required to bring all the relevant documents to the nearest help center on the assigned dates for verification.
  • Collect the online verification slip after the verification of the documents.

Step 3: Selection will be done on the basis of the marks secured in the following exams:
  • MET
  • JEE Mains
  • MP Pre MCA
  • PE and PT
Step 4: Applicants are required to pay the admission fee through online or by DD.

Mandatory Documents
  1. 10th and 12th Marksheets
  2. Graduation Marksheet
  3. Passing Certificate
  4. Migration Certificate
  5. Caste Certificate
  6. Domicile Certificate
  7. JEE/MAT/GATE scorecard
  8. Passport size photograph
  9. Photocopies of all the documents
